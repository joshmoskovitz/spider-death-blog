About

Spider Death Blog is a blog about a small, sympathetic, slightly pathetic spider named Spidey who dies in whimsical, theatrical ways.

Each post features a crude MS Paint illustration of Spidey's demise, accompanied by a deadpan first-person confession of how it happened. The humor lives in the contrast between the playful, punny setup and the matter-of-fact description of a very tiny murder.

The blog started in February 2012 on Tumblr with 9 original posts. It has recently returned with new episodes, still drawn in the same lovingly crude MS Paint style on a 640x480 canvas.

Spidey is not menacing. He is not monstrous. He is just a small gray oval with stick legs and dot eyes, caught mid-routine when doom strikes.

Original blog: spiderdeaths-blog.tumblr.com
